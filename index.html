About us

Some words about us

HIRU ENGINEERING was established in 2014 in order to contribute to the sustainable development of the country by adopting innovation and technology to the construction industry in Sri Lanka. It is primarily aimed Building Constructions and Hiring/Renting of all types of construction equipment to fulfill the needs of construction industry. Hiru Engineering is equipped with fleet of construction machinery, equipment and tools facilitating to undertake any kind of construction projects and to complete timely. The balanced managerial and technical staffs are the core of success gained in all activities. Highly qualified engineers, quantity surveyors, technical officers and supervisors are capable to optimize the investments with proven quality standards.

  • Vission - We are committed to becoming the constructor of choice, pursuing excellence through dedication and experience with an ongoing passion to deliver quality, timely and sustainable projects.
  • Mission - Our mission is to be the leader in construction industry which leads to sustainable development.
